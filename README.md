# A Collection of Everyday Utility Tools

## This is a continuously updated public repository containing tools for (but not limited to) video editing, file management, audio/video processing, and more.

# 1.NormSpect Studio
* NormSpect Studio – A Professional Spectrum Visualization Tool
NormSpect Studio is a browser‑based audio spectrum visualization and video generation tool that transforms any audio file into stunning, customizable spectrum videos.

## 1.1 Key Features
* Audio Import – Supports all mainstream formats including MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A, and AAC.

* Centered Peak Spectrum (Normal Distribution Style) – Unlike traditional left‑to‑right spectrums, the highest bars are positioned at the center, creating a Gaussian‑like, symmetrical visual that places the dominant frequencies in the middle.

* Customizable Quad‑Color Gradient – Freely choose four colors that transition smoothly from left to right across the spectrum.

* Full‑Length Video Export – Generate a complete spectrum video that matches the exact duration of your audio track, not just a short preview.

* Adjustable Background Transparency – A slider and numeric input (0% to 100%) let you make the background fully transparent for overlaying on other videos, or completely opaque black.

* Multiple Resolutions – Choose from popular presets such as 1080p, 720p, 480p, square formats (1080x1080, 720x720), or custom dimensions.

* Real‑time Preview – Play, pause, or stop the audio to see the spectrum respond live to your color, transparency, and effect adjustments.

* Visual Effects – Toggle peak‑hold lines and glow effects for enhanced visuals.

* Progress Indicator – A clear progress bar shows rendering status during video export.

* User‑Controlled Download – Videos are saved manually by the user after generation; no automatic downloads.

* Light / Dark Theme – Switch between light and dark interface themes with one click.

## 1.2 How It Works
* Load any audio file.

* Customize the gradient colors, bar count, transparency, resolution, and effects.

* Click Generate Full‑Length Video – the tool renders a WebM video where the spectrum's peak always stays centered.

* Once finished, click Download to save the video locally.

## 1.3 Ideal Use Cases
* Music visualizers for social media or video projects.

* Background assets for DJ sets or live streams.

* Educational demonstrations of audio frequency analysis.

* Artistic video creation with custom color schemes.

NormSpect Studio runs entirely in the browser – no installation, no server uploads, and no watermarks.
